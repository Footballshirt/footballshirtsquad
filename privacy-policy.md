Privacy Policy — Football Shirt Squad
Last updated: April 3, 2026
Football Shirt Squad ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application Football Shirt Squad (the "App").

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information You Provide

Account information: email address, username, password
Profile information: profile photo, bio, country
Shirt data: photos, club names, seasons, leagues, players, condition, story and other details you add about your shirts
User content: comments, likes, follows, wishlist items

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage data: how you interact with the App, features you use, pages you visit
Device information: device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
Log data: error reports, performance data


2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:

Create and manage your account
Display your shirt collection to other users
Enable social features (likes, comments, follows, notifications)
Improve and develop the App
Send push notifications about activity on your account (likes, comments, follows)
Respond to your support requests


3. How We Share Your Information
3.1 Public Information
The following information is visible to all users of the App:

Your username and profile photo
Your shirt collection (photos and details)
Your likes, comments and followers/following counts

3.2 Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
Supabase — We use Supabase for database storage and user authentication. Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
Google Play Services — The App is distributed via Google Play. Google may collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
3.3 Affiliate Links
The App contains affiliate links to Kitbag (kitbag.com) and Classic Football Shirts (classicfootballshirts.co.uk). When you click these links and make a purchase, we may receive a commission. These third-party websites have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their content or practices.
We do not share your personal data with these affiliate partners.

4. Data Retention
We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.
When you delete a shirt from your collection, it is soft-deleted and not permanently removed immediately, but it will not be visible to other users.

5. Your Rights
You have the right to:

Access the personal data we hold about you
Correct inaccurate data
Delete your account and personal data
Export your data
Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: footballshirtsquad@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy
Football Shirt Squad is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text.

8. Push Notifications
The App may send push notifications about activity related to your account (new likes, comments, followers). You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
